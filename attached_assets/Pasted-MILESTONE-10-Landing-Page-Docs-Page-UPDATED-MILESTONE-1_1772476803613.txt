    - Response Format

    - "How accurate are budgets?" (Teams: within polling interval. Vouchers: per-request, less than $0.05 gap.)
    - "Can I use this with LangChain / LlamaIndex / Cursor?" (Yes — just change base_url.)
    - "What is the difference between Teams and Vouchers?" (Permanent vs temporary. Direct vs proxy.)
