GOAL: After a successful voucher redemption, the recipient lands on
a screen that:

  1. Confirms redemption succeeded (existing UX).
  2. Shows their just-minted key with a one-time-only banner ("Save
     this key — you won't see it again in full").
  3. Shows the four ConnectorCards in a stacked-1-column compact
     mode, pre-filled with the just-minted key.
  4. Shows the same "Test connection" button as /dashboard/connect.
  5. Has a clear "Done — go to dashboard" button that takes them to
     /dashboard/connect (so they can find the same UX again later).
