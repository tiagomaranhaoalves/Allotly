# Piece 1 ‚Äî "Test your key" button on /redeem AND /dashboard/connect

## Why
Every Allotly key user ‚Äî voucher recipient, TEAM admin, TEAM member ‚Äî at some point copies a snippet, pastes it into Cursor/Claude.ai, and *hopes* it works. If it doesn't, there's no diagnostic, and they bounce. This button validates the entire chain (auth + tier + provider + budget) in one click.

Voucher recipients see this on `/redeem` immediately after redemption. TEAM users see it on `/dashboard/connect` whenever they need to verify their setup.

## Locked decisions ‚Äî Piece 1

1. **New endpoint:** `POST /api/v1/test-connection`. Auth: same Bearer key as `/api/v1/chat/completions`. Counts against budget, rate limit, concurrency identically to a real call (because it is one). Universal ‚Äî works for any bearer kind (API key, voucher, OAuth bearer).
2. **Server-side behavior:** the endpoint internally invokes the existing chat completion path with:
   - `model`: cheapest model in the user's allowlist that is currently active across providers
   - `messages`: `[{ role: "user", content: "Reply with the single word 'ok' and nothing else." }]`
   - `max_tokens`: 5
   - `temperature`: 0
3. **Response shape:**
