"Every team using AI has the same moment. Someone asks: 'how much did we spend on API calls last month?' And nobody knows.

We built Allotly because we lived that moment. As engineering leaders, we watched AI adoption explode inside our teams — GPT-4o for code reviews, Claude for documentation, Gemini for prototyping — and loved every minute of it. Allotly Vouchers let you generate shareable codes that give anyone instant AI access with hard budget enforcement. No provider accounts needed. One API key, all models, automatic expiry. Think gift card, but for AI.

Both features, one dashboard. You decide who gets what, for how long, and at what cost."
